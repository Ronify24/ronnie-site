    Lumify Application and Platform
    What it is

    Lumify is an artist-led creative AI application and platform that translates visual intent into governed, inspectable workflows across image, video, 3D, and generated worlds.

    Its HUD-driven control layer coordinates local GPU models and cloud providers in familiar production language—preserving references, masks, camera and lighting direction, session history, cost visibility, and human approval from first frame through studio handoff.
