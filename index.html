Overview

I design and operate AI workflows that accelerate creative and digital storytelling. For the last 19 years, I've been involved with the look development and creative departments in VFX and Animation studios. I love the creation process and thrive off team collaboration. I've always been inspired by visual details, so it is only natural that I have established myself in Look Dev/Surfacing Departments. From assets and locations to world building, I've evolved in my career with the use of Unreal Engine. Concept to creation is the name of the game and Unreal has propelled me to contribute to almost all spectrums of world building and previs production. From assembly, blueprints, to look development, I've found my new passion in the next generation real‑time creative pipeline.
